About

A bit about me and this blog.

Welcome to Chalkboard – a space where I explore the intersection of software engineering, scientific computing, and thoughtful system design.

Who am I?

I am a software engineer focused on building systems that are simple on the surface and thoughtfully designed underneath.

My long-term direction is toward deep technical clarity—understanding how frameworks, libraries, and platforms actually work, not just how to use them. I am particularly interested in frontend internals, system design, and the practical application of AI in real products. This site exists as a working notebook for that journey.

What is this blog about?

Here you'll find deep dives into:

  • Software architecture and design patterns
  • Web development with modern technologies
  • Scientific computing and data visualization
  • Performance optimization and best practices

The Tech Stack

This blog is built with:

  • Next.js 15 – React framework with App Router
  • Turborepo – Monorepo management
  • Tailwind CSS – Utility-first styling
  • MDX – Markdown with React components
  • Velite – Type-safe content layer
